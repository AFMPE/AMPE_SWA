AFRICOM MISSION PARTNER ENVIRONMENT

The United States Africa Command (USAFRICOM) Mission Partner Environment (AMPE) Impact Level 2 (IL-2) is a commander’s tool that can be utilized to provide ally and coalition forces the ability to collaborate during exercises and enduring operations. The AMPE plays a vital role in delivering a seamless and secure collaboration experience.
